Text Cleaner Pro

Clean and format your text by removing extra spaces, line breaks, special characters, and normalizing formatting. Perfect for preparing text for databases, documents, or web content.

How to Use Text Cleaner Pro

  1. Paste Your Text: Copy and paste the text you want to clean into the input area
  2. Select Options: Choose which cleaning operations you want to apply
  3. Clean Text: Click the "Clean Text" button to process your text
  4. Copy Result: Use the copy button to copy the cleaned text to your clipboard

Cleaning Options Explained

  • Remove Extra Spaces: Converts multiple consecutive spaces into single spaces
  • Remove Line Breaks: Removes all line breaks and newlines from the text
  • Remove Special Characters: Removes non-alphanumeric characters (keeps basic punctuation)
  • Normalize Whitespace: Standardizes all whitespace characters to regular spaces
  • Trim Text: Removes spaces from the beginning and end of the text

Common Use Cases

  • Cleaning text copied from PDFs or websites
  • Preparing data for database import
  • Formatting text for social media posts
  • Cleaning up messy text documents
  • Preparing text for translation services
