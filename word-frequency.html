Word Frequency Analyzer

Analyze the frequency of words in your text to identify patterns, find the most common words, and get detailed text statistics for content optimization and analysis.

Analysis Options:

How to Use Word Frequency Analyzer

  1. Input Text: Paste or type the text you want to analyze
  2. Set Options: Choose case sensitivity, common word filtering, and minimum word length
  3. Analyze: Click "Analyze Frequency" to get detailed word statistics
  4. Review Results: See word counts, frequencies, and most common words

Analysis Features

  • Word Count: Total number of words and unique words
  • Frequency Ranking: Words sorted by occurrence frequency
  • Percentage Analysis: Shows what percentage each word represents
  • Common Word Filtering: Option to exclude common English words
  • Case Sensitivity: Choose whether "Word" and "word" are counted separately

Perfect For

  • SEO keyword analysis and optimization
  • Content writing and blog post analysis
  • Academic research and text analysis
  • Social media content optimization
  • Identifying overused words in writing
  • Market research and survey analysis
